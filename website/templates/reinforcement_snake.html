Reinforcement Learning Snake


You will be able to fill in your own parameters and create a snake that
learns to play the game itself.

Exploration / Prediction

The lower value you choose the quicker the Snake will start to make choises from the prediction model,
but will not explore as much and will not be as efficient in the long-term. The higher value you choose
the more exploration / randomness it will have in the beginning but will be give better predictions in the long-term.
